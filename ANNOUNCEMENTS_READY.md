## 👨‍💼 What Admin Can Do

1. **Post Announcement:**
   - Click "➕ Post Announcement"
   - Fill in title, content
   - Select category: "Announcement"
   - Mark as featured for important ones
   - Click "Publish"

2. **Edit/Delete:**
   - Click ✏️ to edit
   - Click 🗑️ to delete

## 📊 Difference Between News & Announcements

### 📢 Announcements:
- Official school communications
- Important updates
- Policy changes
- Deadlines and schedules
- Emergency notifications

### 📰 News:
- General school news
- Event updates
- Workshop information
- Success stories
- Community updates
